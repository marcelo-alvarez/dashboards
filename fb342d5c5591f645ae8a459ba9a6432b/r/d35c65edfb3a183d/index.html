What the rehearsal does (the workflow being exercised)

1. Populate all 100 realizations × 12 redshifts (lean output, 30-min walltime).

2. Ensemble abundance matching — accumulate the ensemble cumulative number density n(>L_in) per

redshift over *all* realizations, set the SINGLE relationship L_cal(L_in, z) per line, store it as an

HDF5 table artifact. This replaces the earlier 2-realization prototype with the real ensemble-wide

calibration.

3. Apply → final catalogs — add the calibrated luminosity to every mock (rank-preserving remap).

4. Characterize — per line × redshift: ensemble-mean LF, 20 individual realizations, and the 68%/95%

realization-to-realization scatter (24 panels).

Hα is abundance-matched to Nagaraj+2023 (observed Hα+[NII]) at the in-band redshifts (~0.5 < z < 2);

out-of-band Hα and [OII] use the identity map and are labeled uncalibrated.
