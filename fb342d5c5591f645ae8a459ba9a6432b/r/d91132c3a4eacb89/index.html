A2 — v0 calibration (Nagaraj+2023, observed Hα+[NII])

Method: per redshift, measure the cumulative number density of the current L_Hα over the available

realizations, integrate the Nagaraj Schechter target to its cumulative density, and rank-order match

— a galaxy at cumulative density *n* gets the L_cal where the target has the same *n*. Built once,

offline, from realizations already on disk; stored as monotone lookup tables (HDF5) applied by

interpolation at mock-build time.

On closure as a *check*: rank-matching reproduces the target by construction where the sample is

complete, so near-perfect closure is expected — the real validation is completeness (does the mock

populate deep enough to fill the Roman-cut regime; it does here) and, at scale, the **Roman number

densities and realization-to-realization scatter**. Those are the A4 gate.
